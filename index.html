House rules

  • Minimum bet $1
  • S17 Game
    • Dealer must stand on all hands totaling 17 (including Soft 17) or higher.
  • 6 decks
  • Penetration 70%
    • Cards will be shuffled if the number of the remaining cards in the deck becomes less than 30% when a new round starts.
  • Blackjack pays 3 to 2
  • You will win if you get a Blackjack, regardless of the dealer's hand.
  • The dealer will check what his hole card is if you do not have a Blackjack. If he gets a Blackjack, he will reveal it immediately.
  • Surrender means you will give up the round and you will get back half of your bet amount.
