How much are you bringing to play game of blackjack?

  • Minimum bet $1
  • Dealer must stand on all 17's
  • 6 deck
  • Penetration 70%
  • Blackjack pays 3 to 2
    • If your hand is Blackjack, you will win whatever the dealer's hand is.
    • The dealer will check what his hole card is if your hand is not Blackjack. If it makes his hand Blackjack, it will be revealed before your action.
